John Kirby: Nintendo's wildcard.

About:

John Kirby was an American lawyer known for successfuly defending Nintendo during a trial against Universal Studios over the character Donkey Kong.

Before the lawsuit:

Although less known for his early career Mr. Kirby had a very strong start. During 1960 in Mississipi, he uncovered the first documented proof of African-Americans being denied their right to vote.
